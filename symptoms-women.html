As a women

The symptoms of menopause shown below can appear daunting and unpleasant, but there are a number of things you can do to ease and prevent symptoms. By understanding what is happening and being proactive in looking after your body as well as talking to and seeking support from those around you, you can continue to be yourself and enjoy life just as you have always done.

Mood and mental health

Fluctuating hormones can cause mood swings and anxiety, with these fluctuations effecting some women more than others. Many women also report low self-esteem.

Talk to your partner or freinds about how you are feeling. Look at ways to reduce stress, this could include getting more sleep, making changes to your lifestyle or using stress management techniques. Regular exercise has been shown to reduce mood swings.
